Dante's Inferno

- The Purgatory -


INSTRUCTIONS


1. Navigate the purgatory by using the direction buttons.

Your goal is to make your way through the purgatory rooms and find the paradise.

If nothing happens, that means in that direction is a dead-end.

2. Look for interactions within the current room.

Certain rooms have hidden interactions that can be used by swapping to the actions menu.

You can swap by clicking the 'SWAP' button located in the bottom of the screen.

You can swap back to the direction buttons by clicking it once again.

3. Turn on the music for a more enjoyable experience.

The music can be turned on by clicking on the play button located on the bottom-right corner.
