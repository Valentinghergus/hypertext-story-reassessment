THE SLOTHFUL

PASSAGE

Go slow, but go

The angel drops you and Virgil on the passage towards the fourth floor, the floor of the sin of sloth.

He warns that the passage ahead will take as long to reach the end, as slothful one is.

Some never reach the end, as their souls are exhausted from the never-ending trip.
