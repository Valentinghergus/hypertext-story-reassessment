TREASURE ROOM

He who envies others does not obtain peace of mind

You reach a room that contains a grandious treasure chest.

The angel advices you that you should only open it if your soul is free of envy. Those before you that envied the riches they did not have, or the people they could not touch, have gone corrupted with the jealousy of the treasure.

Open the treasure chest
