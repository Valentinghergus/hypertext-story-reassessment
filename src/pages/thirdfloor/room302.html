THE WRATHFUL

HALLWAYS

God's wrath is only the face of love refused

While guided to the third floor of the Purgatory by one of the guardian angels, Virgil explains to you how spiritual love can only grow as more share it.

On this floor, the souls of the dead rid themselves of wrath, the third of the Seven Deadly Sins.

The souls who in their previous life had tortured, punished, hurt, and destroyed through their uncontrollable wrath, are now forced to go through the same pain they had caused.
