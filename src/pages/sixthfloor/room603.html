THE LUSTFUL

TEMPTATION ROOM

Avoid temptation

You move up to the seventh floor, in a room where souls who given into their lust, become slaves of the Purgatory.

You come upon a group of shades who are extolling examples of chastity such as the Virgin Mary and the goddess Diana, then another group whose lust was directed towards the same sex, who tell you the story of Sodom and Gomorrah.

Virgil advices that both of you must leave this room straight away, and walk through the purifying flames.
